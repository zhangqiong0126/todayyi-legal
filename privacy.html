1. 引言

Atop Clouds Limited（公司編號：香港註冊；下稱「我哋」、「我們」、「本公司」）營運「今日宜 TodayYi」流動應用程式（下稱「本應用」、「App」）。本隱私政策說明我哋會收集邊啲個人資料、點樣處理、會唔會分享，以及你有啲咩權利。

使用本應用即代表你同意本政策。如果你唔同意，請唔好使用本應用。

2. 我哋會收集啲乜

2.1 你直接提供畀我哋嘅資料

類別內容用途
登入資料電話號碼（用 OTP 驗證）建立 / 識別你嘅帳戶

風水照片你主動上傳嘅居家或場所照片AI 風水分析

2.2 你授權我哋收集嘅裝置資料

  • 相機（Camera）：只喺你主動點「拍照」按鈕時啟用，用嚟拍風水分析照片。我哋唔會喺後台讀取相機。
  • 相冊（Photos）：只喺你主動「從相冊揀」時讀取你選擇嗰張，唔會掃描整個相冊。
  • 推送 Token：用嚟發每日運勢提醒同訂閱通知（你可以喺設定關咗）。

2.3 自動收集嘅技術資料

  • 裝置型號、作業系統版本、語言、時區
  • App 版本、Crash log（透過 Sentry）
  • 使用行為事件（透過 PostHog；唔包含個人識別資訊）
  • IP 位址（伺服器 log，用嚟防欺詐 + 限速）

我哋唔會收集：通訊錄、位置、麥克風、健康資料、瀏覽歷史。

3. 點樣使用你嘅資料

  • 提供風水 / 命理 / 起名 / 抽籤等核心功能
  • 用 AI 大模型（OpenAI、Anthropic、DeepSeek）生成個性化解讀（你嘅出生日期會作為 prompt 上下文，但我哋唔會將你嘅身份綁定畀第三方）
  • 處理付款（透過 Apple IAP、Google Play Billing、RevenueCat、Stripe）
  • 發推送通知（如你開咗）
  • 偵測同預防欺詐、濫用、攻擊
  • 遵守法律義務

4. 風水照片嘅特別說明

呢樣最敏感，所以單獨講：

  • 照片上傳到我哋伺服器（Cloudflare R2，加密儲存）
  • 24 小時內自動刪除原圖（呢個係系統強制嘅，唔係手動）
  • 分析結果（文字）會喺你帳戶下保留，方便你之後睇返
  • 我哋唔會用你嘅照片訓練模型，亦唔會分享畀第三方做廣告
  • 我哋會將照片發送畀 OpenAI / Anthropic 嘅 Vision API 用嚟分析；佢哋有自己嘅資料政策（一般係 30 日內刪除，唔用作訓練），但你應該意識到呢點

根據香港《個人資料（私隱）條例》（PDPO）同其他適用法律，你有權：

  • 撤回之前畀過嘅同意

  • 定期安全審計，但網絡無絕對安全；如果發生資料外洩我哋會喺 72 小時內通知你

Privacy Policy

Atop Clouds Limited · Last updated: 2026-05-07 · Effective: 2026-05-07

1. Introduction

Atop Clouds Limited (a company registered in Hong Kong; "we", "us", "our") operates the TodayYi 今日宜 mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights.

By using the App you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. What we collect

2.2 Device permissions you grant

  • Camera: Activated only when you tap "take photo". We never access the camera in the background.
  • Photo library: Read only the specific photo you select; we do not scan the whole album.
  • Push token: Used to send daily-fortune reminders and subscription notifications. You can disable in Settings.

2.3 Automatically collected technical data

  • Device model, OS version, language, timezone
  • App version, crash logs (via Sentry)
  • Usage event analytics (via PostHog; no PII)
  • IP address (server logs, fraud prevention + rate limiting)

We do not collect: contacts, location, microphone audio, health data, browsing history.

3. How we use your data

  • Provide core features (fengshui, divination, naming, BaZi readings)
  • Generate personalized readings via large language models (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek). Your birth data is used as prompt context but we do not link your identity to third parties.
  • Process payments (via Apple IAP, Google Play Billing, RevenueCat, Stripe)
  • Send push notifications (if enabled)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and attacks
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Special note on fengshui photos

This is the most sensitive data category, so we treat it separately:

  • Photos are uploaded to our servers (Cloudflare R2, encrypted at rest)
  • Original photos are auto-deleted within 24 hours (system-enforced, not manual)
  • The analysis text is retained under your account for later reference
  • We do not use your photos to train models, nor share them for advertising
  • Photos are sent to OpenAI / Anthropic Vision APIs for analysis; their data policies apply (typically 30-day retention, no training use), but you should be aware

8. Your rights

Under the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account (App Settings → Me → Delete Account; or email us)
  • Export your data (contact us)
  • Withdraw previously given consent
  • Complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data, Hong Kong (PCPD)

Email support@todayyi.hk; we respond within 30 days.

9. Security

  • All transmissions encrypted with HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Industry-standard hashing for any secrets (OTP-based flow stores no passwords)
  • Sensitive tokens stored in Apple Keychain / Android Keystore
  • Periodic security audits. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify you within 72 hours of a known data breach

10. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected a minor's data, we will delete it immediately. Parents / guardians who believe a child has provided data should contact us.
